About Me

I am a geomatics and remote sensing professional with a strong interest in understanding how our environment evolves. My work focuses on analyzing georeferenced data to reveal patterns that support better decision‑making. I enjoy transforming complex spatial information into clear, practical insights. My approach is systematic, logical, and grounded in careful analysis. I explore every angle of a problem before reaching a conclusion. At the same time, I value creativity when traditional methods are not enough. I am particularly interested in applying geomatics to agriculture and environmental monitoring. With climate change reshaping global landscapes, spatial intelligence is becoming essential. Canada is gaining agricultural potential, and precision agriculture will be key to managing it sustainably. I am motivated by projects that combine technology, environmental awareness, and real‑world impact. I work well independently and take responsibility for delivering high‑quality results. I enjoy learning new tools, exploring new datasets, and collaborating with multidisciplinary teams. My goal is to contribute to solutions that are both technically sound and meaningful for society. I am currently seeking opportunities to apply my skills through a summer internship. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about my work and interests.
